About the team.

The Rocket Propulsion Laboratory is a student organization at the University of California, San Diego founded in the Spring of 2017. We are a team of dedicated and talented engineering students with a passion for aerospace design.

The RPL team is always looking to bring on the top talent at UCSD. If you are interested in joining the team, contact us with the button below! We welcome all undergraduate and graduate students currently enrolled at UCSD.
