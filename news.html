Design of Augmented Spark Igniter

Like any good rocket engine, RPL’s “Remove Before Flight” needs a way to get itself fired up and running in a safe and reliable manner. Throughout the history of rocket design and development, a great deal of thought has gone into the method of initiating the controlled explosion that is xthe operation of a rocket engine. A wide variety of creative, complex, and ingeniously simplistic means of accomplishing this have been implemented. From spark plugs and lasers to hypergolic fuels which spontaneously ignite upon contact, ignition methods are diverse and selected upon the needs of the engine.
