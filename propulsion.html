Propulsion

The Propulsion team designs RPL's engines

The propulsion team was the first team in RPL. This team started by creating the initial designs for a small liquid rocket engine; Ariel.


About

The Rocket Propulsion Laboratory is a student organization at the University of California, San Diego founded in the Spring of 2017. We are dedicated to innovating in the aerospace industry, with a particular focus on rockets!
