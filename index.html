ABOUT THE ROCKET PROPULSION LABORATORY

The Rocket Propulsion Laboratory designs and manufactures rockets, jet engines, and other propulsion systems. RPL was founded in the spring of 2017 at the University of California, San Diego. The organization strives to provide a challenging and exciting project for engineering students to develop handson skills.
